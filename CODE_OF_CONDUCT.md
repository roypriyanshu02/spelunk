# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

I want this project to be a welcoming, friendly space for everyone, whether you are filing your first issue or your five-hundredth pull request. I do not tolerate harassment. This policy applies regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

I pledge to act in ways that keep our community healthy, welcoming, and inclusive.

## Standards

To keep our community healthy, I expect members to:

- Use welcoming, inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences. People solve problems differently, and those differences make the project better.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what helps the project and the community.
- Show empathy toward other community members, including both human contributors and automated agents.

I do not allow these behaviors:

- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Trolling, insults, and personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private information (like email or physical addresses) without permission.
- Other behavior that does not fit a professional development space.

## Enforcement responsibilities

As the project maintainer, I clarify and enforce these standards. I will take fair, appropriate corrective action if anyone acts inappropriately, threateningly, or harmfully. This includes editing or removing comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies across all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions. It also applies when you represent Spelunk in public, like using an official project email or posting from a project social media account.

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior, please report it privately by opening a [draft security advisory](https://github.com/roypriyanshu02/spelunk/security/advisories/new) on GitHub under the Security tab. I will review and investigate all reports promptly and fairly, and will respect the privacy of the reporter.

### Conflict of interest and single-maintainer escalation

Because I am the sole maintainer of this project, conflicts of interest can arise if a report concerns me. If you need to report an incident involving me, or if you feel a conflict of interest exists:

- Submit a report indicating the conflict, and request that I delegate the review to a neutral, independent community member or a trusted developer.
- Alternatively, outline the issue in a private discussion or request an independent mediator to handle the communication.
- I pledge to respect your decision to involve a neutral third party and will cooperate fully to reach a fair resolution.

### Enforcement guidelines

I will follow these guidelines to determine the consequence for any violation:

#### 1. Correction

I will issue a private warning explaining the violation if someone uses unwelcome or unprofessional language. I may also ask for a public apology.

#### 2. Warning

I will issue a warning for a single incident or a series of actions. The warning will outline consequences for continued behavior. The person must not interact with anyone involved (including moderators) for a specified period across community spaces and external channels. If they violate these terms, I may issue a temporary or permanent ban.

#### 3. Temporary ban

I will temporarily ban the person from community interaction and public communication for a specified period. They must not interact with anyone involved, publicly or privately, during this time.

#### 4. Permanent ban

I will permanently ban the person from any public interaction within the project community if they show a pattern of violations, including sustained harassment or aggression.
